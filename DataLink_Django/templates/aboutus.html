About Our Student Accommodation Finder

Simplifying Housing for CTU-ARGAO Students

Our Mission

At CTU-ARGAO Student Accommodation Finder, our mission is to revolutionize the way students find their home away from home. We've created a comprehensive system designed exclusively for CTU-ARGAO students, making it easier than ever to discover and secure comfortable, affordable housing options near campus.

Our platform serves as a bridge between students seeking accommodations and local property owners, offering a wide range of options including dormitories, apartments, and boarding houses. We provide detailed information on pricing, amenities, and directions, ensuring students can make informed decisions about their living arrangements.

For boarding house owners, our website offers a unique opportunity for free marketing, allowing them to showcase their properties to a targeted audience of CTU-ARGAO students. Property owners can easily sign up and list their accommodations, increasing visibility and occupancy rates.

To maintain the integrity and safety of our community, access to our platform is restricted to verified CTU-ARGAO students only. This exclusivity ensures that all listings and applications are legitimate and relevant to our student body. The Student Affairs Office (SAO) of CTU-ARGAO serves as the superadmin, overseeing the platform to guarantee a secure and beneficial experience for all users.
